Sandewa Villa offers a spacious 4-star 2-bedroom retreat in Ubud — designed for couples and small families seeking privacy, comfort, and authentic tropical living. The property features a private outdoor swimming pool, lush garden, terrace, and balcony that give you room to breathe and truly unwind.

The terrace is the perfect spot for outdoor dining and leisure, offering a refreshing retreat as the Bali sun sets behind the tropical canopy. Every morning begins with birdsong and garden views — a world apart from the everyday.

With 10 years of hosting experience and a 99% response rate, the host team brings seasoned expertise to every stay, ensuring every detail is in place from the moment you arrive.
