The Villa

Tropical Paradise
Meets Modern Comfort

Welcome to Geya Villa — a brand new 3-bedroom retreat nestled in the heart of Ubud. Surrounded by tropical greenery, this private escape seamlessly blends contemporary style with authentic Balinese charm.

The villa features a harmonious blend of design and natural elements creating a calming atmosphere from the moment you arrive. Each bedroom has its own unique character, with soft lighting, inviting décor, and large glass doors that open directly to the garden — allowing nature to be part of your stay.

Unwind by the private pool framed by lush foliage, share a meal in the open-air dining space, or simply soak in the peaceful atmosphere that makes Ubud so uniquely magical. Rated a perfect 5.0 stars across all categories by guests.
