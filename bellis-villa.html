The Villa

Rice Fields & Jungle Views
from Every Window

Escape to Bellis 68 — an enchanting Balinese villa nestled in the heart of Ubud with breathtaking views of rice fields and lush jungle. This 3-bedroom retreat perfectly blends modern luxury with traditional Balinese architecture and warm hospitality.

Step outside and discover your private paradise. The outdoor space is surrounded by lush greenery, providing a peaceful backdrop at all hours. The open-concept living space seamlessly connects indoors and outdoors, with al fresco dining overlooking the stunning rice paddies.

The master suite features a generous layout with a tranquil sanctuary feel, while the second bedroom is equipped with a luxurious bathtub — perfect for unwinding after a day of exploring Ubud. Just 2.5km from Locavore NXT, one of Asia's 50 best restaurants.
