Escape to Bellis 68 — an enchanting Balinese villa with breathtaking views of rice fields and lush jungle. This 3-bedroom retreat perfectly blends modern luxury with traditional Balinese architecture.

The open-concept living space seamlessly connects indoors and outdoors, with al fresco dining overlooking the rice paddies. The master suite is a tranquil sanctuary, while Bedroom 2 features a luxurious bathtub.

Just 2.5km from Locavore NXT — one of Asia's 50 best restaurants.
